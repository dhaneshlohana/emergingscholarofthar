EMERGING SCHOLARS OF THAR (EST) is an ideal platform founded by the scholars of Sukkur IBA University hailing from Tharparkar and Umerkot.

The main purpose of the platform is to facilitate the aspirants from all corners of Sindh who look for scholarships nationally and internationally. These aspirants will be provided with preparatory materials and guidelines.

Furthermore, they would be introduced to study related websites and YouTube channels in order to make them prepared for different scholarships as most of the scholarships are merit cum need base. To make it practical, EST members will conduct mock test either online or on specific locations in different cities as we have members from all around Sindh.

With the same passion, EST will conduct awareness sessions regarding female education with young and moderate souls of the soil to curb the primitive thinking regarding female education. Similarly, there would also be sessions regarding gender discrimination, communication gap, mutual intetests, copy culture etc. As we would be able to get rid of these evils. After all we all are equal and most importantly humans who always wish for the betterment of fellow humans.

So be there and stay connected to your local members for scholarship updates and awareness sessions or follow us on Facebook.

Our motto is “A walk towards educated society.” This is a non-profit organization and our sole purpose is to provide guidance and awareness regarding education without any political cause and without any caste, creed and religion.
